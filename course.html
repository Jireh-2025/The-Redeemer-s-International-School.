COURSE

Courses We Offer for Secondary School

We provide a wide range of subjects to prepare students for excellence in academics and life skills.

Science

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics to build strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Arts

Literature, History, and Creative Arts to nurture imagination and cultural understanding.

Commercial

Business Studies, Economics, and Accounting to prepare students for the corporate and entrepreneurial world.

Our Facilities

We offer a modern and supportive learning environment with well-equipped classrooms, computer labs, a digital library, science laboratories, sports grounds, and Wi-Fi access. Our campus ensures comfort, safety, and resources to help every student succeed.

World Class Library

Our world-class library offers a quiet, resource-rich environment for study and research. Equipped with thousands of books, journals, e-resources, and modern facilities, it supports students and staff with everything needed for academic excellence.

Largest Play Ground

The largest playground in our school provides ample space for students to engage in various outdoor activities and sports. It is well-maintained, spacious, and designed to support football, athletics, and other physical training. This open environment encourages teamwork, fitness, and fun among students.

Tasty And Healthy Food

Tasty and healthy food combines delicious flavors with essential nutrients. It includes fresh fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Eating well doesn’t mean giving up on taste — with the right ingredients and cooking methods, food can be both enjoyable and good for your body.
